Foundational Design of Financial Reconciliation Product

Client: Malbec Project

A leading financial product vendor based out of Jersey City sought me out to design a trade reconciliation product. Working with difficult domain knowledge and vague expectations, I was able to successfully deliver a 1.0 product design from scratch in less than 8 weeks.


The project began with extensive research. In a low-data environment, I conducted SME and stakeholder interviews, and sat in on multi-day team discovery sessions in order to gain understanding of a complex domain.
